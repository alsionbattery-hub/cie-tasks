# Chips Joint Undertaking

[Back to README](../README.md)

Chips JU supports European semiconductor research, innovation, pilot lines, and industrial collaboration. For SDU-CIE, it is most relevant where semiconductors meet power electronics, converters, packaging, reliability, and advanced manufacturing.

## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Wide-bandgap power converters.
- Semiconductor packaging and thermal reliability.
- Power module testing and accelerated lifetime.
- Industrial electronics manufacturing.
- AI-enabled test, design, or diagnostics for power devices.

## Proposal Positioning Notes

Chips JU projects are consortium-heavy. SDU-CIE should normally aim for a focused technical role unless a strong European partner network is already in place.

High-value roles:

- Converter and application demonstrator.
- Reliability testing and validation.
- Power-electronics use case owner.
- Manufacturing and test methodology contributor.
