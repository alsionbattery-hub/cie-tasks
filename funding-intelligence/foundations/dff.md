## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Fundamental converter failure mechanisms.
- AI methods for physical energy systems.
- Battery degradation and lifetime modeling.
- Semiconductor devices and packaging science.
- Control theory for cyber-physical energy systems.

## Proposal Positioning Notes

DFF proposals should be framed around scientific excellence:

- What is the unresolved research question?
- Why is the method novel?
- Why is the applicant/team uniquely able to solve it?
- What is the contribution beyond one application domain?
- How will results change the field?
