# Energy Cluster Denmark

[Back to README](../README.md)

Energy Cluster Denmark is useful for fast, applied collaboration with Danish energy companies. It is especially helpful as a stepping stone toward EUDP, Innovation Fund Denmark, or Horizon Europe proposals.

## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Fast industry scoping around energy storage.
- EMS/BMS prototype validation.
- Industrial microgrids and flexible demand.
- AI-enabled energy optimization.
- Partner discovery before a larger proposal.
