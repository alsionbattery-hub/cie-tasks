## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Maritime BESS and electric propulsion support systems.
- Converter-heavy energy demonstrators.
- Industrial microgrids and flexibility.
- Battery safety, lifetime, and control.
- Energy management systems connected to real operating data.

## Proposal Positioning Notes

EUDP proposals should make the development and demonstration logic explicit:

- What energy technology is being developed?
- What barrier prevents deployment today?
- What will be demonstrated, where, and by whom?
- How does the project contribute to Danish climate and energy goals?
- What happens after the demonstration?
