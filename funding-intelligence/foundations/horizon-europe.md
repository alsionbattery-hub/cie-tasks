# Horizon Europe

[Back to README](../README.md)

Horizon Europe is the main EU framework programme for international research and innovation. SDU-CIE should treat Horizon Europe as a strategic consortium arena, especially for batteries, smart energy systems, maritime electrification, industrial electronics, and AI for energy.

## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Battery and BESS integration.
- Smart energy systems and digital twins.
- Maritime electrification and port energy systems.
- Advanced manufacturing for electronics.
- Semiconductor-enabled power conversion.
- Cybersecure energy infrastructure.

## Consortium Strategy

| Role | When SDU-CIE Should Pursue It | Evidence Needed |
| ---- | ----------------------------- | --------------- |
| Coordinator | Topic directly matches CIE core and existing partners are strong | EU project management capacity and committed consortium |
| Work package lead | Topic needs power electronics, EMS/BMS, AI-energy, or testbed expertise | Clear technical contribution and staff capacity |
| Task partner | Topic is adjacent but strategically useful | Low-overhead contribution and networking value |
