# Innovation Fund Denmark

[Back to README](../README.md)

Innovation Fund Denmark is one of the strongest Danish matches for applied, industry-facing SDU-CIE research. It is especially relevant when a project can combine excellent engineering research, Danish industrial value, and a credible path toward demonstration or commercialization.

## Best-Fit CIE Themes

- Modular and grid-interactive BESS.
- EMS/BMS for industrial and maritime energy systems.
- Wide-bandgap converters and power-electronics reliability.
- AI-enabled diagnostics, forecasting, and control.
- Cybersecure energy infrastructure.
- Commercialization of research-based hardware or software IP.

## Proposal Positioning Notes

Strong IFD proposals usually need:

- A clear societal or industrial challenge.
- Committed Danish industry partners.
- A convincing value chain from research to impact.
- Measurable technical milestones.
- A plan for exploitation after the grant.
