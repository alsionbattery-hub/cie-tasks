## Commercialization Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Invention disclosure or IP review completed.
- [ ] Clear user problem and target segment.
- [ ] Evidence from lab prototype or simulation.
- [ ] Freedom-to-operate risks identified.
- [ ] Industry mentor or first customer interview completed.
- [ ] Next funding route selected: InnoExplorer, Energy Cluster, EUDP, IFD, Horizon Europe.
