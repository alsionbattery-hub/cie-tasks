## Strategic Fit Notes

Best candidates for large grants:

- Already have a named PI and at least one committed industry partner.
- Can articulate a Danish or European strategic need.
- Combine research excellence with credible demonstration.
- Have a clear exploitation path: standardization, product, pilot, spinout, or follow-on project.
