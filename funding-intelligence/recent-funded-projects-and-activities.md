This page tracks relevant funded projects, public project activities, infrastructure launches, and strategic research activities from the latest rolling 3-year window.

Current window: **2024-2026**. At the start of each year, archive entries older than 3 years or move them into the university-specific pages if they remain strategically useful.

## Why Track This

- Spot which funders are actively supporting themes close to SDU-CIE.
- Reuse successful project framing in new proposals.
- Identify active competitors, collaborators, and potential consortium partners.
- Keep proposal ideas grounded in recent funding evidence.

## Annual Maintenance Rule

At each January update:

1. Set the new rolling window, for example `2025-2027`.
2. Keep active projects even if they started earlier than the window.
3. Remove or archive inactive items older than 3 years.
4. Add newly funded Danish and EU projects from AAU, DTU, AU, SDU, and key industry partners.
5. Link every entry to an official source or university project page.
