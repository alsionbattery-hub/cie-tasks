| Stage | Recommended Grant Type | Output |

| Partner validation | Energy Cluster Denmark | Proof of concept and user need |
| IP maturation | InnoExplorer | Commercialization plan |
| Demonstration | EUDP / IFD Grand Solutions | Full project proposal |
