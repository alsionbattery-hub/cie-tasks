## Funding Pattern Notes

- AAU often frames power electronics as an enabling technology for renewable integration, energy efficiency, and grid reliability.
- Strong proposals combine converter hardware, control, reliability, and industry-facing tools.
- High-value CIE takeaway: connect CIE equipment, local industry, and demonstration cases into a recognizable platform narrative.

## Reusable Positioning Language

| Pattern | CIE Adaptation |
| ------- | -------------- |
| Converter reliability as system-level energy infrastructure | Position CIE converter reliability work as essential to BESS, maritime, and industrial microgrids |
| AI for next-generation power electronics | Use AI for diagnostics, design acceleration, EMS/BMS optimization, and predictive maintenance |
| Demonstration with multi-kW prototypes | Build EUDP / IFD proposals around lab-to-field demonstrators |
