## Funding Pattern Notes

- AU often frames AI as a cross-disciplinary method for complex physical and energy systems.
- AU power electronics language emphasizes advanced control and future transportation systems.
- CIE can use these examples to sharpen proposals where AI is embedded in real hardware, rather than only system modeling.

## CIE Opportunity Angles

| AU Pattern | CIE Adaptation |
| ---------- | -------------- |
| AI for future energy systems | AI directly connected to converters, EMS/BMS, hardware faults, and industrial operation |
| Zero-emission hybrid ship EMS | Maritime BESS demonstrator with industrial partner and test data |
| Digital twins for energy modeling | Digital twins for physical electronics and energy assets |
| Semiconductor materials science | Wide-bandgap devices and packaging for converters |
