## Funding Pattern Notes

- DTU often wins through system-level framing: infrastructure, models, sector coupling, and national facilities.
- CIE can differentiate by making the hardware-control interface concrete: converters, BESS, EMS/BMS, testing, and demonstrators.
- DTU examples are useful when building Horizon Europe work packages around digital twins and smart energy systems.
